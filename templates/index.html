Federico Carrone's Blog

I am Federico Carrone, an entrepreneur at the intersection of technology, finance, and culture. Through Ergodic Group, I build and hold companies in distributed systems, cryptography, AI, gaming, and real assets. I also created LambdaClass, a software company focused on protocol engineering, systems programming, and formal verification.

This site is where I write about the systems that shape the future: cryptographic infrastructure, institutional design, and the culture that emerges from both. I focus on what compounds over time rather than what trends today.
